Eric is an American based, amatuer photographer and digital creator focused on improving is skills by documenting his adventures through his lens. He loves to travel and reflects this in his work by sharing the sights and memories from his trips.

Professionally, Eric recently graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science with minors in Data Science and East Asian Studies and currently works as a Developer in Ohio.

Some hobbies of Eric's outside of photography include: motorcycles, cars, video games, reading, mixology, and starting projects he will not finish.
